Here are 15 non-business ideas that could apply to anyone (easy/cheap to build):

• Personal X research assistant - sends you a report every morning
• Financial audit/tracker - tracks your income/expenses into a custom app
• Job application tracker - monitors job boards and auto-fills applications
• Fitness data aggregator - pulls stats from all your health apps into one view
• Fitness coach - optimizes your current plan into one app/dashboard
• Travel deal monitor - tracks flights/hotels for your destinations, alerts on price drops
• Reading list manager - saves articles to read later, sends digest summaries
• Subscription auditor - monitors all recurring charges, alerts you to price increases
• Nutrition tracker - scans meal photos, logs macros, and suggests healthier swaps
• Sleep quality analyzer - aggregates sleep data, correlates with daily habits, and recommends improvements
• Home maintenance scheduler - tracks appliance ages, schedules filter changes, and service appointments
• Prompt library - custom dashboard for tracking your most used/effective prompts
• Prompt optimizer - a tool that automatically optimizes any prompt you send based on the model used
• Habit streak monitor - tracks daily habits across apps, visualizes progress, sends motivation
• Daily journal/notes hub - journal for work, hobbies, gym, reading, etc. - organized into one central hub, then have AI scan for patterns, things to improve, etc.
